No Baking Required Cookies

The only thing getting baked for this recipe is you! Full of flavor and fun, and in no-time done! TheseTreats won't trick when the munchies attack!

Indredients

  • 1 stick of melted butter
  • 1 Cup of melted semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 3 Cups of oats
  • 3/4 Cups of Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 1/8 tsp of Vanilla

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine melted butter and melted semi-sweet chocolate chips, creamy peanut butter, sugar and vanilla.
  2. Mix together until smooth.
  3. Add oats.
  4. Mix together to well combined.
  5. Using a spoon, drop blobs onto a wax sheet covered baking sheet.
  6. Let sit until hard.
