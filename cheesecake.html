The Easiest Cheese Cake Recipe

This easy delicouse Cheesecake is easy enough to bake to please those not baked enough to care and those who care about baking too much!

Ingredients Cheese Cake Filling

  • 3 8oz packages of Cream Cheese, brought to room temperature.
  • 1 cup of Sugar
  • 1/2tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Cup of Sour Cream
  • 3 Large Eggs

Ingredients for Crust

  • 1.5 Cups of Gahram Cracker crumbs
  • 3/4 Tbs Cinamon
  • 3/4 Tbs of Nutmeg
  • 1/8 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 tsp of All Spice
  • 1 stick of Melted Butter

Instructions for Crust

  1. In a mixing bowl combine all your dry ingredients and mix thouroughly.
  2. Mix Vanilla Extract into the Melted Butter.
  3. Drizzle the melted butter/Vanilla Extract mixture into the Graham Cracker crumb/spice mixture, while stiring with a fork.
  4. Once well combined, press the crust evenly into a springform baking pan, making sure to press it well into the bottom edges.
  5. Put aside for now.

Instructions for Cheese Cake Filling

  1. Combine Cream Cheese, Sugar, sour cream and Vanilla Extract into a large mixing bowl. Easiest if you have a food mixer
  2. Mix until smooth and creamy
  3. Add the 3 eggs, one at a time, and mix evenly in.
  4. Pour Cheese Cake filling into the spring form pan and place on a baking sheet.
  5. In an oven preheated to 375F, place the baking sheet on the center rack and bake for 75 minutes. Let sit out for tow hours, befor placing it into the fridge for another 6 hours to finish setting.
