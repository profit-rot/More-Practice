Easiest, and Tastiest Recipe of All!

Have you ever sat down on your couch after a long days work, flicked the bic and burned a bowl, only to come to the realization that you're too stoned to move and have made nothing to eat? Well these recipes are for you, when you want something good, but don't have the focus or the energy for any real baking.

But still you might be wondering what's the easiest recipe, in this long list of easy recipe? What's the tastiest, most mouth watering recipe? It's this one right here.

Ingredients

  • 1 Computer, or Smart Phone, with internet connection.
  • 1 debit, or Credit Card, or other form of internet currency.

Instructions

  1. On your computer or smart phone, open up your preffered search engine, i.e. Safari, Google Chrome, et.
  2. In the search bar, type grubhub.com or seamless.com
  3. In the search bar for your chosen website, search for your preffered food or restaurant.
  4. Once you have found your chosen food, add it to your cart. When you have selected all of your preffered foor, continue to the checkout.
  5. At the checkout, put in the required information from your debit or credit card, or other form of online currency.
    6. And don't forget to give a tip of no less than 20% to your delivery driver.
  6. Wait for delivery. When it arrives, enjoy.
